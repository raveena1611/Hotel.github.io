The hotel offers a wide variety of banqueting venues and a choice of small meeting rooms at 150 square feet each.
The Grand Madras Ballroom, 9200 square feet, is the largest pillarless ballroom in Chennai, with a large pre-function area encompassing a large bar counter serviced through a private entrance.
The hotel has a state-of-the-art business centre, which would provide efficient service for the discerning traveller.
