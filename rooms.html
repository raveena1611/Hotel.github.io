ROOMS & SUITES

Le Méridien offers a truly unique experience in every one of its stylish guest rooms and suites.
Warm earth tones in addition to the luxurious sitting and work areas create an inviting ambience for you to experience and enjoy.

  • SUPERIOR



The standard room category of this hotel, these rooms come with King, Queen and Twin bed options and modern amenities. Facilities like free WiFi*, DVD Player, bathtub, tea coffee maker and an electronic safe makes your stay cozy and comfortable.
Area:261 Sq.Ft.

  • EXECUTIVE



These rooms are well appointed and aesthetically designed to enhance your comfort. They come with King bed and free WiFi* to maximize your in-room experience. Area:287 Sq.Ft.

  • STUDIO



These rooms combine living room and bedroom into a single room with a mini kitchenette. Ergonomically designed for enhanced comfort, these rooms come with King bed, other modern amenities and free WiFi* to make your stay comfortable and relaxing. Area:360 Sq.Ft.

  • EXECUTIVE SUITE



A contemporary ambience and a separate living room as well as a bedroom, these suites are tailor made for greater comfort. Enjoy a range of amenities and conveniences like free airport transfers, in-room microwave and free WiFi* for your personal comfort. Area:479 Sq.Ft.

  • DIFFERENTLY ABLED ROOM



This room is specially designed for our special guests. Equipped with wide doors for the room and bathroom, a seating chair in shower cubicle, a lower height for the vanity counter and water closet, all make for a convenient stay for the differently abled. Area:500 Sq.Ft.
